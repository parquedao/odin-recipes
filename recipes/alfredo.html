Alfredo

Description

This is a delicious recipe for Alfredo sauce that will turn out rich and creamy.

The recipe only requires a few ingredients and can be stored for several days in the fridge or up to three months in the freezer!

Ingredients

  • butter
  • heavy cream
  • parmesan cheese
  • garlic
  • fresh chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Melt butter over low heat.
  2. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cheese and garlic. Whisk until heated through.
  4. Stir in parsley and enjoy.
